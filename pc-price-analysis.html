Case Study

Problem

Product prices were hard to compare because the data came from different shops and locations.

What I did

  • Cleaned raw pricing data.
  • Standardized categories, shops, provinces, and manufacturers.
  • Created a SQL structure for analysis.
  • Built dashboard views for comparison.

Tools used

SQL, MySQL, Power BI, and GitHub.

Key outcome

The project made it easier to identify lower cost products, compare shops, and spot price differences by category and province.
