Local Internet Service Provider Plan Comparison – Small Town Case Study (South Africa)

- This project evaluates the value-for-money of ISP packages offered to residents and businesses in a small South African - town, using objective price and performance metrics to guide better decisions. + This project helps people compare internet plans in a simple and practical way. + It shows which plan gives the best value for money, based on speed and monthly cost.

Project Overview

- This analysis compares ISP packages available in a small South African town to determine whether long-standing ISP - choices truly deliver the best value. Using publicly available data, the project evaluates fibre and wireless - offerings across download speed, monthly cost, installation cost, and overall cost efficiency. The outcome is a - decision-ready view of value-for-money that does not rely on internal business data. + This project compares internet plans from providers that serve one small South African town. + It is designed for residents, small businesses, and anyone who wants to avoid overpaying for internet. + The main purpose is to make plan choices clear, fair, and easy to understand.
