About

I am a student majoring in Information Science, Organizations, and Industrial Psychology, and I am deeply interested in how information, technology, systems, and human behavior intersect. That focus shapes how I approach problems: understand the people and the process, then design the data and tools that make decisions clearer.

I have a strong foundation in IT support, networking fundamentals, cloud basics, security awareness, and data analytics. My skills span end-user support, systems understanding, cloud concepts, security principles, and data-driven thinking, so I can connect what users experience to what the systems are actually doing.

I also bring practical, industry-aligned credentials: Cisco IT & Networking badges for support and infrastructure, a Cisco Data Analytics badge for insight work, Microsoft AZ-900 and SC-900 for cloud and security fundamentals, and the Google IT Support Certificate for hands-on troubleshooting foundations.

I am building toward roles in IT support, networking, cloud, cybersecurity, and data analysis, with a strong understanding of people and organizations.
