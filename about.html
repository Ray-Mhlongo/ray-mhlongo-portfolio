About

I am an aspiring data analyst with a disciplined approach to solving business problems through evidence. I focus on understanding the question first, then structuring data so the final analysis is accurate, traceable, and useful for decision-making.

My work emphasizes analytical thinking and practical problem-solving. I break complex datasets into clear components, test assumptions, and communicate findings in a way that supports action rather than just reporting numbers.

Technically, my strongest focus is SQL, Power BI, and Excel. SQL is central to how I clean and model data, Power BI helps me translate results into clear visual narratives, and Excel supports fast validation and exploratory analysis.

I am building this portfolio to reflect real project workflows—defining the problem, preparing reliable data, surfacing key insights, and presenting outputs that can support operational or strategic decisions.
