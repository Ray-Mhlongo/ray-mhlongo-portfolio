Cathdel Creamy - Retail Ice Cream Sales Analysis (Jan-Oct 2025)

This project analyzes R5 and R6 ice cream sales to deliver actionable insights that help the business owner optimize stock, pricing, and promotions.

Key Features of the Dashboard

  • Monthly profit line chart comparing R5 and R6.
  • Summary table showing total profit per product and best/worst months.
  • Forecast table with average monthly profit per product.
  • Actionable insights with recommendations for stock and promotions.

Excel Formulas Used

Revenue = Units_Sold * Unit_Sale_Price
Cost = Units_Sold * Unit_Cost
Profit = Revenue - Cost
Average Monthly Profit (R5) = AVERAGEIF(Product_Column, "R5", Profit_Column)
Average Monthly Profit (R6) = AVERAGEIF(Product_Column, "R6", Profit_Column)

Outcome

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into clear, actionable insights so the owner can make confident, data-driven decisions.
