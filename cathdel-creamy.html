Case Study

Short version: small daily sales can become useful business insights when captured clearly.

Problem

A small business needs a simple way to track sales, understand customers, and see which products are performing.

What I Did

  • Designed a mobile-first sales capture flow.
  • Created customer tracking logic for repeat purchases.
  • Added loyalty and customer activity concepts.
  • Outlined dashboard metrics for sales and product performance.

Tools Used

Sales Data Dashboard Design Customer Analysis Workflow Planning

Key Outcome

The project shows how a small business can turn daily transactions into clear views of revenue, best-selling products, and customer activity.

Key Features

The system is designed around the questions a small business owner would ask every day.

Sales Tracking

Capture product, quantity, payment method, and time of sale.

Customer Insights

Track customer activity and identify repeat buyers.

Daily Reporting

Summarize revenue, orders, products sold, and payment methods.
