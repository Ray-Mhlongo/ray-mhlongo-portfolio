Cathdel Creamy

Sales analysis project for an ice cream business using real sales data to explore performance trends, product demand, and revenue patterns.

Project Overview

The project examines transactional data to identify top-performing products, demand shifts over time, and opportunities to improve revenue consistency.

Problem Statement

The business needs structured visibility into what drives sales and when. Existing reports do not connect product demand, timing, and revenue outcomes clearly.

Tools Used

  • SQL
  • Excel
  • Power BI

  • Placeholder: A small group of items contributes a large share of revenue.
  • Placeholder: Periodic dips reveal opportunities for promotional planning.
