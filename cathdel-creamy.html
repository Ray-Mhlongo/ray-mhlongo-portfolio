Completed Project

Sales, Loyalty & Business Analytics System

This project is a mobile-first business management system designed to help small businesses track sales, manage customers, and generate practical business insights from everyday transactions.

The system combines sales tracking, customer management, loyalty automation, and business intelligence dashboards in a single web application that runs on phone, tablet, and desktop.

Data confidentiality note: all customer records shown in this ad are synthetic demo data.

Project Goal

The objective was to demonstrate how data collection, automation, and analytics tools can be used to help small businesses make data-driven decisions in daily operations.

Sales Tracking

Staff can quickly capture sales transactions through a simple mobile interface. Each transaction records product, quantity, payment method, and time of sale, enabling accurate revenue and transaction records.

Customer Management

Customers are registered with key details such as name, grade/class, and contact number. Each customer receives a unique QR code for fast identification at purchase.

Loyalty Program Automation

The system tracks purchase behavior and automatically applies a configurable loyalty program to reward repeat customers and support retention strategy.

Business Intelligence Dashboard

The dashboard visualizes core business metrics including:

  • Revenue
  • Number of orders
  • Units sold
  • Customer activity
  • Product performance
  • Sales trends over time

Data-Driven Insights

The system analyzes sales history to identify:

  • Best-selling products
  • High-value customers
  • Sales trends
  • Inventory risks
  • Loyalty program performance

WhatsApp Customer Outreach

Built-in targeting tools prepare behavior-based WhatsApp outreach for segments such as inactive customers and loyalty-ready customers, enabling practical data-driven marketing.

End-of-Day Reporting

Daily reporting is generated automatically with:

  • Total revenue
  • Orders processed
  • Products sold
  • Payment methods used

Technologies and Skills Demonstrated

  • Data analytics
  • Business intelligence dashboards
  • Data visualization
  • Customer data management
  • Sales data tracking
  • QR code systems
  • Workflow automation
  • Mobile-first web application design

Project Objective

The purpose of this project was to show how analytics and automation can be applied to real business operations. By turning transaction data into clear dashboards and actionable insights, small businesses can understand performance faster and make stronger operational decisions.
