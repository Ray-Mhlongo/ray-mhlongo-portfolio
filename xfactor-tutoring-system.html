Project Case Study

X-Factor Tutoring System

An education operations workbook that combines student learning data with payments, expenses, and parent communication logs.

Confidentiality note: this case study uses demo or anonymized data for portfolio purposes.

Problem

Tutoring businesses can struggle when student progress, attendance, and payment data are kept in separate places and cannot be analyzed together.

Solution

I built a full-cycle tutoring workbook that links students, lessons, payments, communication notes, and performance records in one system.

What Data I Tracked

  • Student register: grade, school, subjects, guardian details
  • Lesson logs: attendance, duration, topic, homework
  • Payment records: invoice number, amount, balance
  • Parent communication log: date, method, discussion topic
  • Business expenses related to tutoring operations

What I Did as a Data Analyst

  • Modeled relationships across students, lessons, and payments
  • Cleaned records for consistent grade, subject, and date formats
  • Tracked attendance and payment behavior by cohort
  • Compared academic trends against lesson participation
  • Built report-ready summaries for student and business performance

Main Metrics

  • 50 students enrolled
  • 98+ lessons logged
  • 58 parent communication records
  • Outstanding balances by student
  • Attendance vs performance trend

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Cohort analysis
  • Education analytics
  • Business reporting

Business Impact

The tutoring team gets a clearer view of student outcomes and business health at the same time. This helps with intervention planning and cash-flow control.
