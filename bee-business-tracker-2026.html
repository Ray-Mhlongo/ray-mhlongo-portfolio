Project Case Study

BEE Business Tracker 2026 - Rental Revenue & Client Analytics

A rental operations analytics system that transforms client transactions into clear revenue and cash-flow insights.

This project demonstrates how operational data from a hive-rental business can be structured and analyzed to improve revenue visibility, client management, and financial reporting.

Using Excel-based data modeling, I developed a system that captures rental transactions, automatically generates invoice references, and aggregates monthly revenue across clients.

The goal was to convert manual rental records into structured business analytics that help the company monitor revenue performance, client activity, and outstanding balances.

Confidentiality note: this case study uses demo or anonymized data for portfolio purposes.

Problem

The hive rental business previously relied on manual tracking of rental transactions, invoices, and payments across separate spreadsheets.

This made it difficult to answer important operational questions such as:

  • Which clients generated the most revenue
  • Which invoices remained unpaid
  • How rental activity changed month to month
  • What the total revenue performance looked like over time

Without structured reporting, the business lacked clear visibility into client activity and cash flow.

Solution

I created a rental operations analytics workbook that integrates client records, rental transactions, invoicing, and revenue summaries.

The system captures each rental transaction in a structured format and automatically calculates totals across months and clients, providing clear reporting on revenue performance and outstanding balances.

Data Organized and Tracked

  • Client Database: client names and company details, location information, contact details
  • Rental Transactions: rental date, hive quantity rented, rental rate per hive, payment method
  • Invoice Tracking: automated invoice ID generation, invoice value and payment status, outstanding balances per client
  • Monthly Revenue Records: monthly transaction summaries, aggregated rental revenue, client-level contribution by month

Data Analyst Responsibilities

  • Designing structured transaction capture fields for reliable monthly data
  • Creating automated invoice numbering using conditional logic
  • Using cross-sheet formulas (SUMIFS / COUNTIF) to aggregate revenue data
  • Segmenting clients based on rental activity and revenue contribution
  • Analyzing transaction patterns to identify cash-flow trends

Key Metrics Tracked

The demonstration dataset includes:

  • 50 active clients
  • 50 rental transactions recorded for March
  • Automated invoice tracking

Key analytical outputs include:

  • Top revenue-generating clients
  • Monthly rental demand patterns
  • Outstanding invoice balances
  • Client revenue contribution analysis

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Data structuring and table modeling
  • Cross-sheet formulas and aggregation
  • Revenue reporting
  • Client analytics
  • Business intelligence reporting for SME operations

Business Impact

By consolidating rental transactions and invoicing into a structured analytics system, the business can quickly monitor client activity, revenue generation, and payment status.

This improves financial visibility and enables better follow-up on unpaid invoices while helping the business identify its most valuable clients.
