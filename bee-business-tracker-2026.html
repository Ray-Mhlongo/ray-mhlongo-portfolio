Project Case Study

BEE Business Tracker 2026

A hive-rental business tracker that links client activity, rental transactions, invoicing, and revenue insights.

Confidentiality note: this case study uses demo or anonymized data for portfolio purposes.

Problem

The hive rental business needed a simple way to track client rentals, invoices, and balances without manual reconciliation across multiple files.

Solution

I built a workbook that captures each rental transaction, auto-generates invoice IDs, and rolls client revenue into monthly summary sheets.

What Data I Tracked

  • Client master data: names, locations, company details, contact info
  • Hive rental transactions: date, quantity, rate, payment method
  • Invoice numbers and amounts outstanding
  • Monthly totals from March to December sheets
  • Client-level revenue contribution and payment status

What I Did as a Data Analyst

  • Standardized transaction capture fields for clean monthly records
  • Used conditional formulas for invoice ID generation and balance tracking
  • Built SUMIFS/COUNTIF cross-sheet aggregation for monthly and client totals
  • Segmented clients by value and activity
  • Reviewed demand and cash-flow patterns

Main Metrics

  • 50 active clients in dataset
  • 50 March transactions loaded
  • Automated invoice tracking
  • Top revenue clients by month
  • Outstanding balance monitoring

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Cross-sheet formulas
  • Client analytics
  • Revenue reporting

Business Impact

The business can now monitor who is renting, who has paid, and which clients drive the most revenue. This supports better pricing and follow-up decisions.
